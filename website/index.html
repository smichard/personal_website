Dr. Stephan Michard Test

Stephan is a Senior Systems Engineer working at Dell Technologies with a focus cloud stacks and developer experience. Born and raised in Cologne his interest for technology and computer science lead him to pursue studies of electrical engineering and information technology at the University of Aachen and at the University of New South Wales in Sydney. Stephan is an outcome oriented IT consultant with years of experience in developing both software and hardware solutions for complex environments. A systems engineer with an interest in Cloud Native app development, he is a strategic thinker who has worked closely with various companies to help develop their digital business strategies and refine their IT agendas. Stephan enjoys learning about the latest technology and listening to tech podcasts. He built graphR. a tool which automatizes and simplifies the analysis of RVTools exports.
